# Ideate

Generates creative solutions and explores possibilities for: $ARGUMENTS

Uses a subagent or Task() to apply structured ideation cards to the current problem space.

## Workflow

1. **Frame the challenge** — clarify what problem or opportunity is being explored
2. **Go wide** — explore across domains; subconscious pattern-matching finds the most generative connections
3. **Apply ideation cards** — use the deck from the lib to guide structured divergence
4. **Track threads** — record every idea; do not shut down threads prematurely
5. **Identify resonance** — notice which ideas produce emotional signal (excitement, curiosity, unease)
6. **Synthesize** — group related ideas, identify 3–7 candidate directions for further exploration

## Key Techniques

- **Read the Temperature**: if a direction feels blocked, reframe the question rather than push harder
- **Go Wide and Deep**: connect disparate domains before drilling into one
- **Thread Discipline**: keep idea threads spooled — return to them rather than abandoning them
- **Optionality Homeostasis**: aim for 3–7 strong options; too many means too few constraints
